Online streaming content guide

Business objective

Nieuw Deze Week offers visitors an overview of newly added movies, series, audio books, ebooks and music for popular streaming services. Backoffice planning tool

Business objective

A client in the real-estate photography business was looking for way to more efficiently manage the scheduling of photo shoots to keep up with the growth of its business and team of photographers. The previous proces would involve the manual booking of appointments, which was error-prone and made monthly invoicing a cumbersome task. In addition to automation, there was a need to add a confirmation workflow to reduce no-shows.

Execution

Created a Python Django application with custom admin screens to provide a fully customized booking screen that is optimized for mobile as well as desktop. To keep technical maintenance to a minimum, Heroku was selected as the PaaS provider. On creating and updating a project, through the use of APIs the system automatically updates the photographer's calendar and creates a uniform project folder in the team's Dropbpox. The client then receives an email asking them to confirm the booking, which is logged in the system. On completion of the assigment, the system sends an email with a download link to the client, and the system integrates with a 3rd party invoicing tool to allow the assigment to be invoiced.

Results

Fully eliminated discussions around no-shows and forgotten appointments. 95% reduction in time spent on administering appointments.

Sup Rondje Haarlem event and registration website

Business objective

Sup Rondje Haarlem is an annual paddleboarding race and tour in the city of Haarlem. A website was need to offer greater visibility. The abilility to register beyond a simple form was a welcome addition to reduce manual tasks by the event organisers both leading up to the event and on race-day.

Execution

With a traffic peak shortly before the event, a scaleable soltion with minimal overhead was called for. We settled on a solution using Node which builds a static website on deployment that is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, combined with a lightweight D1 database and Workers for the registration and payment process.

Results

Building static pages during deploys, combined with cloud services provides the highly scalable Cloudflare plafform, while the lack of servers leads to zero hosting costs during the off-season.
